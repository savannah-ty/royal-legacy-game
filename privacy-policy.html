Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

🔒 Overview

Royal Legacy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our match-3 puzzle game application.

Key Point: We prioritize your privacy by storing all data locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal information to external servers or sell it to third parties.

📊 Information We Collect

Local Storage Data

Our application stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game progress and level completion status
  • In-game currency (gold) and resources
  • User preferences and settings
  • Card collection album data
  • Daily sign-in streak information

Technical Data

We may collect limited technical information necessary for the application to function:

  • Device type and screen resolution (for responsive design)
  • Browser type and version
  • Application crash logs (stored locally)

🎯 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To save and restore your game progress
  • To provide personalized gaming experience
  • To maintain your in-game inventory and achievements
  • To improve application performance and fix bugs

🔐 Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored exclusively on your local device using browser LocalStorage technology. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No internet connection is required to play
  • You have full control over your data
  • You can clear your data at any time through the application settings
Important: Clearing your browser data or local storage will reset your game progress. We recommend using the in-app backup features if available.

🚫 What We Don't Do

We are committed to your privacy and want to be clear about our practices:

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not track your activity across other websites
  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We do not collect payment information (all transactions are handled by third-party payment processors)

👶 Children's Privacy

Our application includes age verification features. Users under 13 years of age are restricted from accessing certain features, including in-app purchases. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

✏️ Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the application
  • Modification: Update your preferences and settings anytime
  • Deletion: Clear all data through the Settings menu
  • Portability: Export your game data (if feature is available)

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the application after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

📞 Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application's support channels or settings menu.
